Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy of our applications

Last update: 21 February 2018

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for our App's available on "Google Play Store" and "Apple Store". For us, user privacy is very important. Before use our App's read this privacy policy.


1. Application

All our App's are built using Unity Technologies Software.


1.1 What data are collected while you using our App's?

While you use our App's, Unity Technologies may collected some or all of the following information:

  • Unique device identifier generated from the device MAC/IMEI/MEID, which Unity Technologies alter to limit the ability to identify the relevant device in the future;
  • An advertising ID, such as the Apple IDFA or the Android Ad ID;
  • IP address;
  • Device manufacturer and model;
  • The operating system and version running on your system or device;
  • Browser type;
  • The make of the CPU, and number of CPUs present;
  • The graphics card information, such as type, vendor and driver name, version and graphics API;
  • Amount of system and video RAM present;
  • Current screen resolution;
  • Unity Player and Unity Editor versions;
  • Operating system identifier (e.g., Mac, Windows, etc.);
  • A checksum of all the data that gets sent to verify that it did transmit correctly;
  • App ID of the game installed.

Unity Technologies primarily use the information collected to provide, administer, operate and improve their Services.


1.2 Analytics and Advertising services

In our App's we don't uses any Analytics or Advertising services.


1.3 Content ratings

All our App's content is considered suitable for all age groups.


2. Your Consent

By using our applications, you consent to our privacy policy. You always have the option to refrain from using the App's or to discontinue using the App's if you do not want that Unity Technologies collected information about you.


3. Changes to our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change this policy at any time. We suggest you to check this page regularly so that you check for any changes.


4. Privacy Question

If you have questions concerns about our privacy policy please contact us: iannibelli.n@gmail.com
